What I Overheard On My Way Home - First Draft

See, I told you,
It's all the same.
Must you keep ignoring our bane?

Oh, come on,
It's not so bad.
Get through this and we'll again be glad.

You don't understand,
You're a disc on a record.
Away with you, bring in the liquor.

You go too far!
Do you not seek clarity?
We will go nowhere without solidarity.

Look at his mind,
It can't take much more.
The advice you're giving is making it worse.

There's no other way,
What you seek is vengeance.
There is no one to blame; the answer is patience.

Just turn him loose!

I won't allow that abuse!

Can't you see that you're inviting the noose?!

You two are quite rowdy,
I wish you both had a switch.
The world is already cloudy,
Must you give me an itch?

I can still see the road,
But my headlights grow dimmer.
With this thousand-pound load,
Hope is but a glimmer.

Now shut up for a while,
Home is just around the corner.
They must not join my trial;
My inner disorder.

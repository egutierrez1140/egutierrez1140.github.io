Aesthetic Statement - abstract Poem()

Being a Computer Science major, I have written many, many scripts and files, both for class and for personal projects. Programming is my true passion, and near the end of the semester, I decided to try to funnel that passion into a poetic piece. My goal was to make a poem that could, in theory, be compiled and run on a machine. This ended up being a tougher task than I initially anticipated, so I decided to take creative liberties and make the code ALMOST be able to compile. I just wanted to experiment in a style that I have not seen before. I doubt I'm the first person to try this kind of poem out, but, to my knowledge, I am the first to try this. I need to Google this once I have some after classes and finals are over.

Anyway, I was mainly concerned with exploring a new style for this piece, not necessarily with any big or important meaning to get across to the reader. I just chose the general topic of poems and their meanings and essentially let my mind ramble, just to see what would appear on screen. The style is based on C++ code, one of my personal favorites.

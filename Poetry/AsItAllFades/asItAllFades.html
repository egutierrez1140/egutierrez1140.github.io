Aesthetic Statement - As It All Fades

I tapped into my inner depression to write this piece. I wouldn't say that I have a severe case of it, but this last semester has not been very good for my overall health, especially not for my state of mind. So, I decided to channel all of my negative and perhaps hyperbolic thoughts into a poem. I tend to take a darker tone when I write poetry, and I enjoy a darker tone anyway, so it all works out for me. This poem was a good start for me to get the ball rolling with poetry, and I actually opened up to the genre way more than any of the others.

Specifically, this poem was written as I thought about my last semester in college, and how it could have broken me. I was lucky enough not to end up like the character in the work (at least not completely) due to those close to my heart, and I can't thank them enough for that.

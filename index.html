Welcome to my E-Portfolio for LAIS 201. Right off the bat, you might be wondering, "Why is it titled English++?" I think most people should get the joke, but in case you don't, no worries, and no judgment. One of the most popular programming languages out there is C++. As a soon-to-be-graduated Computer Science major, I am very familiar with the programming language. Throughout my programming career in college, though, I have somewhat lost touch with perhaps the most important language at my disposal: English.

So, in a way, taking this class was like exploring another programming language; learning syntax, interpreting expressions, and figuring out what each "program" does. Hence the name "English++." Whether you are my instructor who is going through this site to grade my portfolio, or you somehow, inexplicably, randomly stumbled upon this page, I hope you enjoy your time here, and I hope you can find some enjoyment in my amateur English works.
